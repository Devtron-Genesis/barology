About Us

The inability to have a child affects around 6.7 million women in the United States every year. To enhance their chances of becoming pregnant, many women and men take 7 or more different pills each day.

The Barology team set out to change that. At Barology, we wanted to make fertility nutrition simpler. We wanted to make it better. By creating a bar that encompasses all these nutrients and tastes great, we did just that.

Our Mission

Barology fertility is focused on a strong commitment to helping couples achieve pregnancy through elegantly simple, cost-effective, vitamin-packed and doctor formulated products based in science and nutrition. Our mission is to provide a convenient one-stop bar that offers the same fertility boosting qualities as multiple vitamins and dietary supplements, making these nutraceutical goals both more accessible and achievable

Who We Are ?

Jill Spatz, RN

Jill Spatz is a registered nurse with expertise in the field of fertility. After graduating with a Master's Degree from Rush University in Chicago, Jill began her career at the Fertility Centers of Illinois, where she worked with world renowned reproductive endocrinologist, Dr. Brian Kaplan. While helping patients achieve their dream of having a child, Jill noticed an increasing focus on the use of a blend of vitamins and supplements to help support egg quality and overall fertility in men and women. Often, this meant taking multiple supplements daily. Because most of those going through fertility were already taking multitude of medications, Jill wanted to make the process easier and more convenient. Thus, the Barology bar was born, as a perfect combination of science and nutrition. Both women and men can receive vitamins and dietary ingredients in a convenient, great-tasting bar.

Why?

At Barology, we know how exciting (but stressful) starting a family can be. So many vitamins and supplements exist to support reproductive health, but the sheer volume and amount can be overwhelming. Enter the Barology Fertility Bar, designed to make the process as simple and stress-free as possible.

Barology Fertility Bars contain specialized fertility supplement protocols designed in collaboration with a renowned fertility specialist, and are based on current medical literature. Studies have suggested that these vitamins, minerals and antioxidants can potentially help against oxidative stress, and therefore help egg and embryo quality, as well as sperm quality, DNA and function.~ The recommended vitamins and dietary ingredients are listed in the chart below (and are found in our bars!).
